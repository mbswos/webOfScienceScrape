Research Interests


Broad research interest is in the use of accounting information by investors and financial analysts. Areas of current research include analyst forecasting behavior, earnings management, industry effects on firm profitability, role of revenue information in evaluating earnings quality, and the effect of industry growth on firms’ future revenues and profits.
